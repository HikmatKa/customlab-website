Who We Are

Built from the ground up
in Saudi Arabia

CustomLabX is a Saudi-based custom sneaker studio founded in 2025 by a small passionate team who saw something missing in the market. Custom painted sneakers had taken off internationally — but nobody was doing it properly in Saudi Arabia. So we built it from the ground up.

We work exclusively on Nike Air Force 1s, transforming blank canvases into one-of-a-kind wearable art — all by hand, no prints, no shortcuts.

What We Do

Your vision.
Our hands.

We take your vision and paint it onto a pair of Air Force 1s. Every design is custom — meaning nothing we create is ever made twice. Whether you have a clear idea or just a feeling, we work with you from start to finish to bring it to life.

We handle every order through Instagram, where you can see our work, reach out, and place your order directly.
